Draws are held at Atlanta, Georgia, every 1st Day of Month at 11:00 p.m., Eastern Time. The draw machines and sets of Tickets , each are randomly selected and certified by outside auditors, to ensure that tickets and equipment allow each number an equal chance of being drawn. Each Lottery draw Ticket set used goes through a series of pre-draw tests to ensure the absolute randomness of the results. Two draw officials who are lottery employees and one member of an independent auditing firm administer tests and conduct/observe the draws. All draw equipment is securely maintained with limited access to draw officials only. The entire process takes approximately two hours, for a draws that takes less than 60 seconds.
